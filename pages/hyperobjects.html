◢ Bacteria Can’t See Beyond Their Bodies
◢ Bacteria are invisible to the naked eye.
◢ They lack any sort of external information gathering capabilities.
◢ They have no way to perceive anything beyond their bodies.
◢ You can only see the bacterial colonies on a Petri dish through a microscope.
◢ The Petri dish is a sort of visual obscura , a device that displays an image within a cube.
◢ A device that displays an image within a cube. The cube is simply an abstract way of exhibiting images within a sphere, or other four-dimensional space.
◢ in a ball, or other four-dimensional space. The sphere is simply a three-dimensional representation of a four-dimensional space
◢ How did the bacteria come to see the Petri dish differently than you do?
◢ How did the bacteria come to see the Petri dish differently than you do?
◢ The Petri dish does not exhibit any sort of visual perception.
◢ The Petri dish is simply the vessel of exposure.
◢ Nothing is at a different distance from the Petri dish than it is from the bacteria.
